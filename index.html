UC Faculty on Admissions Testing — Survey Response Structure

Provisional results of a survey of UC faculty on undergraduate admissions policy · n = 320 respondents · June 26, 2026
Disclaimer. Faculty were sampled at random within two groups — those who signed the open letters and those who did not. Which of the roughly 1,500 invited faculty actually replied was not mine to control: 320 did, for reasons I have not modelled. The figures are weighted by signatory status, to offset the deliberate oversampling of signatories, but they are not adjusted for whatever else distinguishes these 320. They are accordingly best read as suggestive — dependable as to the direction and rough size of differences, less so as to exact levels. No individual responses are shown; the data appear only in aggregate, with the smallest groups withheld.
The explorer contrasts a subgroup of respondents with the sample as a whole. Numerical values remain hidden until a bar is selected.
